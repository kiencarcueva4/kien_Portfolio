About Me

I'm Kien Carcueva, and I live on the stunning island of Manukan. It's a place that feels like home to me. When I'm not exploring the beauty of this island, you'll probably find me cruising on my motorbike. There's something special about the thrill of the ride, the wind in my hair, and the freedom it brings.

Aside from bikes, I'm also a huge fan of books whether it's getting lost in a great story or learning something new, I love a good read. Animals hold a special place in my heart too; they bring so much joy and love to life. I'm also a sucker for breathtaking views and have a soft spot for gazing at the moon it just never gets old.

And when I'm not out riding or reading, you'll likely find me in the kitchen, experimenting with new recipes. Cooking is a form of relaxation for me, and there's nothing quite like sharing a delicious meal with others.
